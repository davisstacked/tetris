TETRIS 

The goal of tetris is to rotate and drop pieces to create full lines. 
When a line is full of pieces, the pieces will disappear and the score increases.
Above 100 points, the level will increase and so will the speed.
When the play area is full of pieces the GAME ENDS and remaining pieces disappear.

KEYS:

To ROTATE pieces use keys Q (rotate left) and W (rotate right).
To MOVE FROM SIDE TO SIDE use the Left and Right arrow keys. 
To DROP piece use the down arrow button.
